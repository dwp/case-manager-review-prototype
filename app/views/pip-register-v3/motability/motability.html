The next question is about the Motability Scheme

The Motability Scheme lets disabled people use their mobility payments to lease a car, scooter or powered wheelchair.

The scheme is operated by a not for profit organisation and is regulated by the Motability Charity.

If you're eligible, they can send you a leaflet in the post, with information about the help they offer.
