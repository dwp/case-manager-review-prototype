Do you agree that your health and care professionals can share relevant information about your health conditions to support your claim?

The Department for Work and Pensions (DWP), or assessment providers approved by the Secretary of State, may need more information or evidence to prcess and make a decision on your claim.

How DWP uses this information

If you agree, DWP will store your consent on your behalf. This means your health and care professionals will know they are legally allowed to share information about you.

DWP needs information about your health condition and how it affects you.

The law allows DWP to get, keep, and use this information for the purpose of processing and making a decision on your claim and on any mandatory reconsideration or appeal that you've made.

If you change your mind

You can change your mind at any time. If you do, DWP will not ask your health and care professionals to share any more information.

If you want to give or withdraw your consent later, contact:
