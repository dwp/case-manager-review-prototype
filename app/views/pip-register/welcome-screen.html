Apply for Personal Independence Payment (PIP)

Use this service to:

  • make a claim for PIP
  • describe how your health conditions or disabilities affect your ability to do certain everyday tasks and get around
  • upload any supporting evidence you have of your conditions or disabilities and how they affect you

Before you start

You do not have to answer all the questions in one go. You can save your progress at any point and continue later.

The date you submit this first stage will be the date of claim. The sooner you do this, the earlier your payments could begin if you're awarded PIP.

For the first stage you will need:

  • your address and contact details
  • the name and address of your health and care professionals, and
  • your bank or building society account details
